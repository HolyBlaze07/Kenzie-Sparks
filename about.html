Meet Kenzie Sparks

A creative young woman using art to reflect beauty, simplicity, and thoughtful living.

Hi, I’m Kenzie!

I’m a 23-year-old digital artist and journal lover who enjoys turning everyday moments into visual stories. I created Sparks of Wonder to share encouragement, creativity, and joy through art.

When I'm not designing, I love sipping tea, sketching outdoors, or reflecting through writing. My work is rooted in peace, simplicity, and delighting in the little things.

Fun Facts About Me

  • 🌿 Favorite Tea: Lavender Earl Grey
  • 🎨 Favorite Tool: Procreate on iPad
  • 🍂 Favorite Season: Autumn
  • 📖 Current Obsession: Repurposing notebooks
“Through simple art and thoughtful design, I hope to point others to rest, joy, and the beauty of intentional living.”
