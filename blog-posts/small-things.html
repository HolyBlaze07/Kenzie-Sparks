5 Ways to Delight in the Small Things

Posted on July 5, 2025

In a world that chases big wins, God gently invites us to slow down — to pay attention to the small, quiet places where His presence dwells.

Sometimes the greatest blessings aren’t in major milestones, but in the tiny details: sunlight through a curtain, the comfort of a warm drink, a page of quiet notes.

1. Notice and Name

Start by noticing what’s already around you. Write it down. Say it aloud. Thank God for it. “The steadfast love of the Lord never ceases...” (Lamentations 3:22–23) — and His mercies show up in the smallest ways.

2. Capture the Moment

Take a photo. Sketch a quick doodle. Record what made you smile today — not for an audience, but as a quiet praise.

3. Practice Gratitude Daily

Keep a list beside your bed or in your planner: 3 little things you’re thankful for. You’ll start to see God’s kindness woven through your ordinary routines.

4. Let Go of Performance

You don’t need a perfect environment to be joyful. Contentment is learned, and joy is a fruit of the Spirit. The Lord delights in our obedience and awareness — not our hustle.

5. Worship Through Your Routine

Whether it’s making tea, designing a planner page, or folding laundry — do it with Him. Invite Him into everything, and even the smallest acts become sacred.

God is in the details. The more you notice, the more you’ll realize you’re never walking alone.
